Hear About our Guru! / ಗುರು ಪರಿಚಯ

For the devotees who are engaged in worshipping me (Parameshwara) constantly without any expectations, I will take care of their well being i.e yogakshema. This has been said by Lord Krishna in Bhagavad Gita chapter-9 and 22nd verse. Yogakshema Yoga Center has also the same purpose. Yogakshema yoga center gives good health (physical, mental, intellectual, spiritual, and social well-being) to the ones who come seeking good health. Along with teaching asana, pranayama, and meditation for well-being, our center teaches various subjects like our mythology, culture, scientific influences, the ideas of the great men, the achievements of ancient India and modern India, the stories of the great men, personality development, social responsibilities, the treks, and travels. Yogakshema Yoga Center now has branches at Rajajinagar and Nagarbhavi running different batches with flexible timings. Along with also have 4 online batches. In addition, there are also weekend Bala Samskara Kendra (yoga for children), weekend yoga teacher training courses, and weekend special yoga sessions. It is not an exaggeration to say that all these are possible because of various members' support and involvement. Yogakshema yoga center is just not run by one person, it is an energy-based center with all your cooperation.
